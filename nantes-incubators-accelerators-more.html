Nantes incubators, accelerators & more

Thomas Loiret
  Fri 22 September 2017

Disclaimer: All information bellow are not ordered, I don't have any preferences about startups, incubators, etc.

Introduction

I follow Nantes startup ecosystem for quite a while and lately I heard about so many new structures to help and support startups, just this year there is at least 4 new incubators! I decided to list them all in order to have a better understanding of Nantes ecosystem.

If there is any error, don't hesitate to ping me on twitter.

Incubators

  • 1kubator
    • Website: 1kubator.com/incubateur-nantes/
    • About: Incubation of 10 months. 25k€ funding. In exchange of 10% equity.
    • Startups: Take a desk, Switch up, Makidoo, ...
    • Key guy: Sandrine Charpentier
  • IMT Atlantique incubator
    • Website: incubateur.imt-atlantique.fr/home-en/
    • About: Within a high-level engineering school.
    • Startups: Meyko, Sensorwake, Shopopop, ...
    • Key guy: Régine Drouet Delhomme
  • Maia Mater
    • Website: www.maiamater.camp
    • About: Takes no equity! Free room and board. For first-time entrepreneur, just graduated or students
    • Startups: abradebarras, LocoMiam, Familicy, ...
    • Key guys: Quentin Adam, Terence Desclain
  • Novapuls
    • Website: novapuls.fr
    • About: Start first semester of 2018. Incubation of 12 months. 20k€ funding. In exchange of 1 to 2% equity. Subsidiary of Sodero Gestion (french bank).
    • Startups: –
    • Key guy: Amélie André
  • Eco Innovaition Factory
    • Website: www.atlanpole.fr/eco-innovation-factory/
    • About: Incubation of 6 months. Works closely with Atlanpole
    • Startups: EKO Verde, Akajoule, Velco, ...
    • Key guy: Samuel Bachelot
  • Octopus
    • Website: –
    • About: Manged by Lengow.
    • Startups: Dictanova, Younboost Market, Kaemo, ...
    • Key guy: Mickael Froger
  • Centrale-Audencia-ensa incubator
    • Website: innovation.audencia.com/entreprendre/incubateur-centrale-audencia-ensa-nantes/
    • About: Managed and works with severals high rank schools.
    • Startups: 10-vins, Hydrocean, Innosea, ...
    • Key guy: Aurélie Couapel
  • Labo des Ecossolies incubator
    • Website: www.ecossolies.fr/L-incubateur
    • About: Incubation of 12 months.
    • Startups: Work and co, Envie 44, Le reflet, ...
    • Key guy: Guillaume Leroux
  • Harmonie Mutuelle incubator / Creative Care Factory
    • Website: -
    • About: Focus on startups working on care, health, well-being... Co-manged by Harmonie Mutuelle and the SAMOA.
    • Startups: Atmotrack, Team officine, Mobidys
    • Key guy: -
  • NMcube / Nouveaux Médias, Nouveaux Modèles à Nantes Métropole
    • Website: -
    • About: Focus on emerging medias. Co-managed by Ouest Médialab, Creative Factory, Audencia and Polytech Nantes. Starting in january 2018 with a batch of 10 startups.
    • Startups: -
    • Key guy: Julien Kostrèche?

Accelerators

  • ADN'Booster
    • Website: www.adnbooster.fr
    • About: Acceleration of 6 months.
    • Startups: 3D Counting, Teester, Moderato, ...
    • Key guy: Anthony Gongora
  • Le Village by CA - Atlantique Vendée
    • Website: www.levillagebyca.com/village/le-village-by-ca-atlantique-vendee/
    • About: Acceleration of 2 years. Part of network "Le village by CA" created by the french bank Crédit Agricole.
    • Startups: Semoa, Stamp.yt, Enerpro, ...
    • Key guy: Nathalie Massé
  • Imagination Machine
    • Website: www.imagination-machine.com
    • About: 3-month program. 15k€ funding. In exchange for 6% equity. Silicon Valley DNA + the creative spirit of Nantes
    • Startups: –
    • Key guy: Rob Spiro
  • Millésime
    • Website: millesime.1kubator.com
    • About: Focus on wine tech. In exchange of 5% equity. Managed by 1kubator and La Vigne Numérique.
    • Startups: –
    • Key guy: –
  • OFF7
    • Website: off7.co
    • About: Acceleration of 12 months. Managed by press group Ouest France. Within the Startup Palace.
    • Startups: Kadran, Score n'co, Mediego, ...
    • Key guys: Fabrice Bazard, Florian Hervéou
  • Operation Elephant
    • Website: www.operationelephant.fr
    • About: 5 months pre-accelerator. Within the Startup Palace.
    • Startups: Smart My Data, Team officine, Eqwall, ...
    • Key guy: Florian Hervéou
  • Startup Palace accelerator
    • Website: accelerateur.startup-palace.com/en
    • About: Focus on raising money up to 1M€. Works with the fund Go Capital
    • Startups: –
    • Key guys: Antoine Dumont, Mathieu Le Gac, Benoit de la Celle
  • Creative Factory Selection
    • Website: https://www.creativefactory.info/creative-factory/
    • About: Between 6 to 9 months acceleration following by 2 years of coaching.
    • Startups: Serigrafball, Emoko, Garage Sanka
    • Key guys: Jean-Luc Charles, Fabrice Berthereaux, Sophie Maitrallain
