Marketplace, the difference between offers and product

  Thu 15 June 2017

TLDR; ??

When we talk about marketplace from a client point of view, those are website with a list of products. The client doesn't care how everything works behind, he can just buy products. But when you look at it from a merchant point of view or from the marketplace's administrator point of view, it can be little bit more subtile. Let's take too example to understand how its works.

Case 1: eBay

On eBay, as a merchant, you create item you want to sell and that's it, whatever is your product, you have it online. What you see then online is your produt with all the informations that you prodivde.

Case 2: Amazon

On Amazon, as a merchant, you must provide information for your product including an EAN (its important for later). Then your product goes online but with some informations that are yours and some not.
